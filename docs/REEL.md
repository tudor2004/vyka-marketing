## Voiceover script (paste into CapCut → Text-to-speech, Romanian voice — or record)
1. Așa arată camera ta acum.
2. Și așa o transformă Vyka.
3. Fiecare piesă e reală — din IKEA, JYSK, Mobexpert și Beliani.
4. Încarcă o poză. Prima ta cameră e gratis. Vyka punct ro.

## CapCut steps
1. **New project**, canvas **9:16**. Import all assets.
2. **Before**: place `ee_before.jpg`, 2.0s. Animation → **Zoom in** (slow). Add text „Așa arată camera acum." (top third).
3. **Transition** before→after: tap the join → **Whip** (or **Zoom**), ~0.3s. Put it ON the music beat.
4. **After**: `ee_veo_9x16.mp4`, trim to ~5s. **Mute the clip's own audio** (it has Veo ambient). Text „…și așa o vede Vyka." → after 2s swap to „Reimaginată în câteva minute."
5. **Shoppable**: `ee_shop_9x16.png`, 5s. Optional: animation **Zoom in** slight; or cut 4 quick „pop" scale-ups timed to ticks.
6. **CTA**: `ee_hero_9x16.png`, 4s. Hold; small **pulse** on „gratis".
7. **Voiceover**: add the 4 lines via TTS (Romanian) or record; place each under its beat. Duck music to ~25–30% under voice.
8. **Music**: Audio → search „before after", „satisfying", „aesthetic house", or „cozy lo-fi". Pick one with a **drop at ~2s** and align the drop to the transition.
9. **Captions**: CapCut → Captions → Auto (reads the VO) OR manual. Keep them short, bottom third, 1 line.
10. **Export**: 1080×1920, 30fps, high quality.

## Brand styling (match vyka.ro)
- Text font: clean sans (CapCut „Aileron" / „HK Grotesk" approximate Inter); titles can use a serif (look for „Playfair"-like) to echo Fraunces.
- Colors (hex): cream **#FBF7F0**, ink **#15110D**, **terracotta #C25E37** for the one highlighted word, muted **#6B6155**.
- Keep it calm and uncluttered (Quiet Gallery) — one text line at a time, generous space, no flashy stickers.

## Tips for "captivating"
- The **first 1s must show the dated room + a clear promise** — viewers decide to stay here.
- The **transition on the beat** is the dopamine hit — don't skip the audio sync.
- The **prices** in the shoppable beat are the scroll-stopper (proof it's real + affordable).
- End on the **free-first-design CTA** while the logo + vyka.ro are on screen.
